About Us

A South African team that loves trees.

Kudzai Tree Felling and Pruning was founded with one simple promise — to deliver safe, reliable and affordable tree services to homes and businesses across Pretoria and surrounding areas.

Our Story

Built on trust, grown by referral.

What started as a small two-person crew has grown into a full-service tree care company serving hundreds of households and businesses across Gauteng. We've stayed family-run on purpose — it's how we keep our standards high and our service personal.

Whether it's a single overhanging branch or an entire row of mature trees, our experienced arborists bring the right gear, the right training and the right attitude to every job. Most of our work comes from word-of-mouth, and we're proud of that.
